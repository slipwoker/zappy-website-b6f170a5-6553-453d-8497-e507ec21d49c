Posting Guidelines

Respectful Language:Please use kind and considerate words when sharing memories. Honor Avraham's legacy with thoughtful expressions.

Privacy Matters:Avoid sharing private family details or sensitive information. Keep messages focused on shared memories and public moments.

Keep It Concise:Brief, heartfelt messages are most meaningful. Aim for 2-3 sentences that capture your memory or blessing.

Community Spirit:This space is for collective remembrance. Messages that uplift and unite are most welcome.

Appropriate Content:Please ensure all posts are suitable for all ages and backgrounds visiting this memorial.

Corrections & Removals:If you need to edit or remove a message, please contact us directly. We're here to help maintain this sacred space.

Technical Issues:If you experience any difficulties posting or viewing messages, reach out to our support team for assistance.
