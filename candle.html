Virtual Candle Lighting

Honor the Memory Through Light

Lighting a candle is one of the most meaningful ways to honor and remember a loved one. Through our virtual candle lighting service, you can participate in this sacred tradition from anywhere in the world. Each candle lit represents a moment of remembrance, a prayer, and a connection to the eternal soul of Avraham Yosef Tamam z"l. Follow the simple steps below to dedicate a candle and share in this beautiful mitzvah.

Request a Candle Dedication

A simple way to participate

Pause and reflect

Take a quiet moment to honor Avraham Yosef Tamam z"l. Reflect on his life, his kindness, and the impact he made on those around him. Let this moment be one of reverence and connection.

What memory brings you comfort today?

Set an intention

As you light your virtual candle, set a personal intention—a prayer, a wish, or a commitment to carry forward his legacy. This act of remembrance becomes a bridge between memory and meaning.

What intention will guide your remembrance?

Share the merit

After lighting your candle, you may choose to share this moment of remembrance with family and friends. Spreading the merit through WhatsApp or Facebook is optional, but it helps extend the circle of honor and community support.

Who else might cherish this remembrance?

Light moments

In the gentle glow of remembrance, we find connection to those who have touched our lives. Each flame represents a moment of light, a memory cherished, and a legacy that continues to illuminate our path forward.

Need help dedicating a candle?

We're here to assist you in honoring the memory of Avraham Yosef Tamam z"l. Whether you need guidance with lighting a virtual candle or would like to add a personal dedication message, our caretakers are ready to help you create a meaningful tribute.
